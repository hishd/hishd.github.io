Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Rickety Rick! This application is designed to provide information about the TV show Rick and Morty by utilizing the publicly available Rick and Morty API (rickandmortyapi). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it. By using Rickety Rick, you agree to the terms of this policy.


Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information about you when you use Rickety Rick. The app is solely for fetching and displaying data related to the Rick and Morty TV show through the Rick and Morty API.


Third-Party Services

Rickety Rick uses the Rick and Morty API (rickandmortyapi.com) to retrieve data about characters, episodes, and locations from the show. The Rick and Morty API is an external service, and the app does not share or transmit any personal data to this service. Please refer to the Rick and Morty API's own privacy policy for information on how it handles data: Rick and Morty API


Local Storage and Tracking

Rickety Rick does not use cookies, local storage, or any other tracking technologies. The app does not store any data on your device beyond what is necessary for its normal functioning, which is limited to displaying data from the Rick and Morty API.
